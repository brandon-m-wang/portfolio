About

Myself

Howdy! I'm a first-year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and Economics. My passion for full-stack development has led me down a journey of developmental self-teaching, and I strive for fluidity in both design and implementation. I'm a self-proclaimed lo-fi producer, mogul skier, k-drama aficionado, squid-lover, rythmist, low-key anime watcher, and Miata enthusiast. From boba lamps to interactive data visualizations, I love building for the sake of building. Let's connect.
